kcylon  0.2
A simple kernel module for controlling 10 LEDs over GPIO in a cylon pattern, and its speed can be controlled by pressing a button. Sysfs mounts these GPIO ports under /sys/class/gpio/ for gpio port 65 as /sys/class/gpio/gpio65 for example.
